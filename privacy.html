Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data NomadDesk collects, how it's used, and what your rights are.

Data we collect

This is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not operate a database or user accounts. The only data collection that may occur is:

  • – Server logs via GitHub Pages (IP address, browser type, pages visited). These are retained by GitHub according to their privacy policy.
  • – Email address if you subscribe to our newsletter (stored securely with our email provider).

Third-party links

This site contains links to Amazon.com. When you click those links, you leave NomadDesk and are subject to Amazon's privacy policy. Amazon may set cookies on your device after you click our links. We have no control over this.

Cookies

NomadDesk itself does not set cookies. Amazon and any analytics tools we use may set their own cookies. You can control cookie behaviour through your browser settings.

Google Fonts

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts, which may log your IP address as part of the font delivery process. See Google's privacy policy for details.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you (limited to your email address if you have subscribed). To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@nomaddesk.example.com.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent change.
