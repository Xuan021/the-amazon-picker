About NomadDesk

Independent since 2024

NomadDesk exists because remote work gear advice is mostly terrible. Review sites are flooded with affiliate farms that haven't touched the products they recommend. "Best of" lists that are just Amazon search results with a star rating slapped on.

We're remote workers ourselves. We've done Zoom calls from airport lounges, co-working spaces in Kuala Lumpur, and tiny hotel rooms where the desk is the size of a dinner tray. We know what actually matters — and what's marketing speak.

How we pick products

Every product we recommend is either one we own, have tested ourselves, or have researched exhaustively from verified buyer communities and professional reviewers we trust.

Our criteria, in order of priority:

  • 1. Does it work reliably, day after day?
  • 2. Is it portable enough for its purpose?
  • 3. Does the price match the value?
  • 4. Is it available internationally?

How we make money

We participate in the Amazon Associates affiliate programme. When you click a link on this site and buy something on Amazon, we earn a small commission — typically 2–5% of the sale price. It costs you nothing extra.

This is how we keep the lights on without running ads or accepting paid placements. We do not accept free products from manufacturers in exchange for coverage. We do not have sponsored reviews. Read our full disclosure.
